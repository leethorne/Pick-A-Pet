Who We Are:

If you're thinking of becoming a first-time pet owner or you're adding another addition to your furry family - we can help! So often, people want to adopt pets in need, but dont know where to begin. Pick-A-Pet teams up with reputable animal adoption agencies in Southern California and Oregon that make owning a pet easy! From the adoption process and beyond, Pick-A-Pet approved shelters will have your solutions. Team up with us to make a healthier and happier world.

Our Mission:

Pick-a-Pet is a unique company with a mission: Saving Pets & Enriching Lives. We connect people with centers close to their home that offer pet adoptions, pet supplies, grooming and dog daycare, all in one location. Our goal is to help reduce pets being euthanized in shelters and offer a way for customers to be a part of our mission. Join us in making a difference for pets and pet owners!
